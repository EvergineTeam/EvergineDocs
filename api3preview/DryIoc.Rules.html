    WithTrackingDisposableTransients()

    Turns tracking of disposable transients in dependency parent scope, or in current scope if service is resolved directly.

    If there is no open scope at the moment then resolved transient won't be tracked and it is up to you to dispose it! That's is similar situation to creating service by new - you have full control.

    If dependency wrapped in Func somewhere in parent chain then it also won't be tracked, because Func supposedly means multiple object creation and for container it is not clear what to do, so container delegates that to user. Func here is the similar to Owned relationship type in Autofac library.
