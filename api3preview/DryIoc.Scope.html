    Dispose()

    Disposes all stored System.IDisposable objects and empties item storage. The disposal happens in REVERSE resolution / injection order, consumer first, dependency next. It will allow consumer to do something with its dependency before it is disposed.

    Remarks

    All disposal exceptions are swallowed except the ContainerException, which may indicate container misconfiguration.
