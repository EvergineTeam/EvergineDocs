    Gets or sets a value indicating whether the physics entity is kinectic or Dynamic.

    Mass

    Gets or sets the mass of the physics entity.

    Remarks

    Setting this to an invalid value, such as a non-positive number, NaN, or infinity, makes the entity kinematic. Setting it to a valid positive number will also scale the inertia tensor if it was already dynamic, or force the calculation of a new inertia tensor if it was previously kinematic.
